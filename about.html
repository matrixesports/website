About Work

What We Do

For Teams

Give fans the ability to invest in your team through earning or buying tokens.
‍
Raise money directly from fans by selling tokens (we’ll handle the legal aspects for you. The SEC has released new regulation to make this simple.)

Gain better fan engagement by allowing fans to own a part of your team. We grant fans the ability to receive dividends, vote, and receive special rewards.

For Creators

Earn money directly from your fans through selling tokens.

Reward your fans for engaging with your content with tokens they can trade with each other or redeem for exclusive experiences such as getting to join a virtual one on one call with you. 

You can also create commemorative tokens for participating in special events such as sponsored tournaments, collab streams, etc.

For Fans

Invest in your favorite teams/creators and potentially earn a profit

Show your support and loyalty for a team or content creator by investing in them and their limited merch

Get exclusive experiences from your favorite teams and creators when you invest

Before

Entertainment was a single-player game.

‍Instead of fan's money going directly to creators, it goes to agencies and private equity firms.
‍
‍We are passive observers in a vacuum, watching content that is fed to us. We're completely separated from what we consume.

Now

Entertainment is a multi-player game.

Matrix bridges the gap between creators and fans.

Now, content creators are able to sell exclusive rewards to their fans, instead of being obligated to sponsors. Now, teams can give their fans power to buy or earn a stake in what content creators produce. 

With Matrix, fans develop a stronger relationship with teams and creators. They’re more engaged. They can actively vote on what they want to see. Fans will now have ownership in the content they consume.
