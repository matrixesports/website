What We Do

For Teams

Raise money directly from fans
Gain better fan engagement by allowing fans to own a part of your team. We grant fans the ability to receive dividends, vote, and receive special rewards

Create a token that represents a share in the team. Fans can buy and trade this token.
    Teams can reward fans with shares for doing various actions (following on social media, watching games, etc.)

For Creators

Reward your biggest supporters with limited merch (digital equivalent of concert merchandise, ticket stubs, etc.)

Provide exclusive experiences (shareholder dinners, 1:1 meetings with creators, etc)

‍Earn money from resales of the merch

For Fans

Invest in your favorite teams/creators and potentially earn a profit

Show your support and loyalty for a team or content creator by investing in them and their limited merch

Get exclusive experiences from your favorite teams and creators when you invest

Before

Entertainment was a single-player game.

‍Instead of fan's money going directly to creators, it goes to agencies and private equity firms.
‍
‍We are passive observers in a vacuum, watching content that is fed to us. We're completely separated from what we consume.

Now
‍

Entertainment is a multi-player game.

‍Matrix bridges the gap between creator and fan.
‍
‍Instead of content creators being obligated to sponsors, they can sell exclusive content directly to their fans. Instead of teams being trapped through ownership by a few rich people, they can be invested in by their fans.

With Matrix, fans can develop a stronger relationship with teams/creators. They will be more engaged. They'll actively vote on what they want to see. The fans own their content.
